# Nexus SaaS – Account Management Guide

## Overview
This document covers everything related to managing your Nexus account — setup, profile settings, password reset, team members, and account deletion.

---

## 1. Creating Your Account

1. Go to `app.nexus.io/signup`
2. Enter your name, work email, and password
3. Verify your email — check your inbox for a confirmation link from `noreply@nexus.io`
4. Complete onboarding by selecting your use case and team size
5. You are now on the **Free plan** by default

> **Tip:** Use your work email, not a personal one — it makes team collaboration and billing easier.

---

## 2. Logging In

- Login page: `app.nexus.io/login`
- Enter your registered email and password
- If you use Google SSO (Enterprise only): click **Continue with Google**

**Forgot your password?**
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (valid for 30 minutes)
4. Click the link and set a new password
5. Log in with your new password

> **Note:** Password reset emails sometimes land in spam. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within 2 minutes.

---

## 3. Updating Your Profile

1. Log in to `app.nexus.io`
2. Click your avatar (top right) → **Profile Settings**
3. You can update:
   - Full name
   - Display name
   - Profile photo
   - Timezone
   - Notification preferences
4. Click **Save Changes**

---

## 4. Changing Your Email Address

1. Go to **Profile Settings → Email**
2. Enter your new email address
3. A confirmation link will be sent to the **new** email
4. Click the link to confirm the change
5. Your login email is now updated

> **Important:** You cannot change your email to one already registered on another Nexus account.

---

## 5. Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Profile Settings → Security**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click **Update Password**

Password requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

---

## 6. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enabling 2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account.

1. Go to **Profile Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

**To disable 2FA:**
1. Go to **Profile Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Disable** and confirm with your password

---

## 7. Managing Team Members (Pro & Enterprise)

**Adding a team member:**
1. Go to **Settings → Team → Invite Member**
2. Enter their email address and select their role (Admin / Agent / Viewer)
3. Click **Send Invite**
4. They will receive an email invitation valid for 48 hours

**Removing a team member:**
1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Find the member and click **Remove**
3. Confirm removal — they lose access immediately

**Changing a member's role:**
1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the member's name → **Edit Role**
3. Select new role and save

---

## 8. Transferring Account Ownership

To transfer ownership to another team member:
1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the target member → **Make Owner**
3. Confirm with your password
4. You will become an Admin; the new owner receives full control

---

## 9. Deleting Your Account

> **Warning:** Account deletion is permanent. All data is deleted after 30 days with no recovery.

1. Export your data first: **Settings → Data → Export All**
2. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**
3. Type "DELETE" to confirm
4. Click **Permanently Delete Account**

Your account is deactivated immediately and fully deleted after 30 days.

---

## 10. Account Locked or Suspended

Your account may be locked if:
- Too many failed login attempts (5 attempts = 15-minute lockout)
- Suspicious activity detected
- Payment failure after 3 retry attempts

**To unlock:**
- Wait 15 minutes and try again (failed login lockout)
- Contact `support@nexus.io` for suspension-related issues
- Update payment method for billing-related locks: **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**
