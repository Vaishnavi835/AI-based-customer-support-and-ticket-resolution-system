# Nexus SaaS – Company Policies

## Overview
This document summarizes Nexus's key policies including privacy, data retention, GDPR compliance, acceptable use, and terms of service. For full legal documents, visit `nexus.io/legal`.

---

## 1. Privacy Policy Summary

Nexus collects and uses your data only to provide and improve the service.

**What we collect:**
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Usage data (features used, ticket volume, API calls)
- Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we never store card numbers)
- Support tickets and Knowledge Base content you upload
- Device and browser information for security purposes

**What we never do:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your support ticket content to train our AI models without explicit consent
- Share your data with advertisers

**Full Privacy Policy:** `nexus.io/legal/privacy`

---

## 2. Data Retention Policy

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Active account data | Retained while account is active |
| Deleted account data | 30 days after deletion, then permanently removed |
| Support tickets | 2 years (Pro/Enterprise), 6 months (Free) |
| API logs | 90 days |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Backups | 30 days rolling |

**To request early data deletion:** Email `privacy@nexus.io` with subject "Data Deletion Request" and your account email.

## 3. GDPR Compliance

Nexus is fully GDPR compliant for customers in the European Union and European Economic Area.

**Your rights under GDPR:**
- **Right to Access** — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Right to Rectification** — Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to Erasure** — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Portability** — Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to Object** — Opt out of certain data processing activities
- **Right to Restrict Processing** — Limit how we use your data

**To exercise any GDPR right:** Email `privacy@nexus.io` with your request. We respond within 30 days as required by law.

**Data Processing Agreement (DPA):** Available for Enterprise customers at `nexus.io/legal/dpa`

**EU Data Residency:** Enterprise customers can request EU-only data storage. Contact your account manager.

---

## 4. CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

**To submit a CCPA request:** Email `privacy@nexus.io` or visit `nexus.io/ccpa`

## 7. Security Policy

**How we protect your data:**
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Regular third-party penetration testing
- 24/7 security monitoring

**Reporting a security vulnerability:**
If you discover a security issue, please report it responsibly to `security@nexus.io`. Do not disclose publicly until we have had 90 days to address it. We offer a bug bounty program — details at `nexus.io/security/bounty`.

## 8. Cookie Policy

Nexus uses cookies for:
- **Essential cookies** — Required for login and session management (cannot be disabled)
- **Analytics cookies** — Help us understand how the platform is used (can be disabled)
- **Preference cookies** — Remember your settings like theme and language (can be disabled)

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

**To manage cookies:** Go to **Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences**

## 9. AI & Data Usage Policy

**How your data interacts with our AI:**
- Your Knowledge Base documents are used to generate answers for your customers only

**Opting out of AI processing:**
Enterprise customers can request a private AI deployment. Contact your account manager.
