# Nexus SaaS – Technical & Error Code Reference

## 1. App Not Loading
If the application is not loading, try these steps in order:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Try a different browser.
3. Disable browser extensions.
4. Check our status page for ongoing incidents.
If the issue persists, contact support with your browser version and OS.

## 2. Data Export
To export your account data, go to Account Settings and click 'Export Data'. A CSV file with all your data will be emailed to you within 24 hours. This includes your ticket history, chat history, and account information. Data exports are limited to once per 30 days.
